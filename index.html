Inaugural Awards

Created for unsigned artists ready to be heard.

The Sound Forward Awards gives unsigned artists in the UK an opportunity to gain recognition and feedback for their music.

Entries are open across single, album, genre and digital creation categories. The awards are designed to celebrate original music, new voices and the changing way music is made.
