About the awards

Created for unsigned artists ready to be heard.

The Sound Forward Awards gives unsigned artists in the UK an opportunity to gain recognition and feedback for their music.

Entries are open across single, album, genre and digital creation categories. AI may be used in any category, provided entrants are clear and honest about how it has supported the work.
