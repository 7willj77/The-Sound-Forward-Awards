The inaugural awards

Built for new music, new artists and new ways of creating.

The Sound Forward Awards 2026 gives unsigned artists across the UK an opportunity to gain recognition and receive feedback for their music.

Whether your track was created in a bedroom studio, recorded with a band, produced digitally, or supported by AI, these awards are designed to celebrate the music moving forward.

AI-friendly awards

AI can be used in any category.

The Sound Forward Awards recognises that music creation is changing. Artists may use AI in any category, whether as part of writing, production, arrangement, artwork, vocals, composition or digital creation.

We simply ask entrants to be clear and honest about how AI was used when submitting their entry.
