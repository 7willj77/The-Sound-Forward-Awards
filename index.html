Award Categories

Best Newcomer 2026

For artists releasing their first track publicly after 01/01/2026.

Best Single

For one standout track released by an unsigned UK artist.

Best Album

For albums with at least 8 tracks.

Best Digital Creation AI

For creative work using AI as part of the music creation process.

Best Country

For unsigned UK country artists and tracks.

Best Dance

For dance, electronic and club-focused releases.

Best Rock

For rock artists, bands and releases.

Best Metal

For metal, heavy and alternative heavy music.

Best Pop

For pop artists, songs and releases.

Best Other

For music that does not sit neatly inside the listed genres.

AI Music Policy

AI can be used in any category. Artists are welcome to submit music that is fully human-created, AI-assisted, or digitally created, provided the entry is submitted honestly and clearly.
